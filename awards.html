Awards & Recognition

We, the community following Baraa Khatib Salkini at Data With Baraa, believe it is time to recognise educators who invest their lives and knowledge to educate others—consistently providing content of the highest quality. We proudly support these prestigious awards to honour Baraa’s profound contributions in bridging economic, social, gender, and geographical divides, educating and inspiring future and current data professionals globally.

🌍 Global Teacher Prize

Deadline: 1 September 2025 (08:00 GMT)

Summary: A US $1 million award celebrating exceptional teachers worldwide who have made an outstanding impact on their students and communities. It seeks visionary educators reinventing learning and changing lives beyond the classroom.

Deadline: Nominations open October 2025 (exact closing date TBA).

Note to Volunteer: Please monitor yidanprize.org in October 2025 for the official 2026 nomination window and add the deadline to your calendar.

Summary: The world’s highest accolade in education, recognising groundbreaking achievements in Education Development—new methods that make learning more widespread and effective. Laureates receive a gold medal, cash prize, and project funding to scale their work.
