Roles That Communicate Directly with the Nominee

Role Communication Responsibilities
Nomination Lead Acts as the primary point of contact. Communicates strategy, decisions, deadlines, and ensures the nominee is supported and informed at every step.
Document & Proof Gatherer Requests personal information, documents, and specific analytics from the nominee. Maintains clear and timely communication to ensure all needed materials are provided.
Content & Endorsement Writer Gathers stories, quotes, and achievements. May conduct interviews or use questionnaires to create compelling content directly from nominee input.
