Cross‑Functional Team Flow

See who are your team members and how to collaborate. We got this!

Team Members Primary Function & Key Collaborations
The Strategy & Eligibility Team
  • Nomination Lead
  • Award Researcher & Eligibility Specialist
  • Content & Endorsement Writer
 Define the "why" and "what"—select the most appropriate awards, confirm eligibility, and outline strategic narratives.

Key Collaboration: Researcher supplies requirements → Writer shapes narrative → Lead ensures alignment with overall goals.
The Content & Collection Team
  • Content & Endorsement Writer
  • Document & Proof Gatherer
  • Nomination Lead
 Create core nomination assets—compelling narratives and supporting evidence.

Key Collaboration: Writer specifies evidence needs → Gatherer supplies data → Lead reviews and approves.
The Submission & Quality Assurance Team
  • Submission & Compliance Officer
  • Document & Proof Gatherer
  • Nomination Lead
  • Content & Endorsement Writer
 Ensure nomination packages are complete, accurate, compliant, and submitted on time.

Key Collaboration: Compliance Officer relies on Gatherer for files and on Writer & Lead for content accuracy.
The Community & Outreach Team
  • Community Engagement & Promotion Specialist
  • Nomination Lead
 Focus on external communication and leveraging community support—strategize promotions, engage the community, and maintain consistent messaging.

Key Collaboration: Engagement Specialist connects with the community → Lead approves messaging.
