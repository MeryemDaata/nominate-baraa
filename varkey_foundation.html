1. Award Summary: Global Teacher Prize

The Global Teacher Prize, a prestigious US $1 million award, recognises exceptional educators who have made an outstanding impact on their students and communities. For a decade, it has celebrated visionary teachers who reinvent learning, tackle local challenges, and change lives beyond the classroom.

  • Eligibility: Current full‑time or part‑time teachers (including online) instructing children aged 5–18 (or 4+ in recognised Early Years curricula).
  • Teaching Time: Minimum 10 hours per week face‑to‑face.
  • Commitment: Intend to remain in the profession for the next five years.
  • Winner Announcement: World Governments Summit, Dubai.

Key Dates for 2025‑2026 Cycle

  • Application/Nomination Deadline: 30 Sep 2025 (09:00 GMT)
  • Top 50 Announcement: Nov 2025
  • Top 10 Announcement: Jan 2026
  • Winner Announcement: 5–6 Feb 2026 (World Governments Summit, Dubai, UAE)

Contact for Questions: info@globalteacherprize.org

Assessment for Nominee “Data With Baraa”

Strengths & Alignment with Prize Criteria

  • Global Reach & Impact Beyond the Classroom: Baraa’s free STEM education on YouTube serves learners worldwide, aligning with the prize’s focus on community impact.
  • Reinventing Learning: By removing financial and geographical barriers, Baraa exemplifies innovative, accessible education.
  • Tackling Educational Inequality: Provides quality STEM learning to underserved populations, addressing a critical global challenge.
  • Dedicated to Education: Meets the “currently working teacher” definition for online courses and demonstrates long‑term commitment.

Areas Requiring Further Information

  • Target Age Group Confirmation: Evidence that a significant portion of the audience is 5–18 years old (YouTube analytics, testimonials, school usage).
  • “Face‑to‑Face” Interaction Proof: Show ≥10 hours/week of live streams, Q&A sessions, or direct feedback with learners.
  • Community Engagement: Demonstrate facilitation of learner interaction, mentorship, or collaborative projects.
  • Impact Metrics: Quantify outcomes—e.g., success stories, job placements, improvements in STEM literacy.

Conclusion

“Data With Baraa” embodies the spirit of the Global Teacher Prize through innovative, high‑quality STEM education that reaches a global audience. With robust evidence addressing age‑range and interaction criteria, Baraa stands as a strong candidate for this prestigious honour.
