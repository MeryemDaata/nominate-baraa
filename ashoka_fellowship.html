1. Award Summary: Ashoka Fellowship

Ashoka builds and nurtures the world's largest network of leading social entrepreneurs, selecting individuals through a rigorous process and providing them with lifelong support. The fellowship is designed to help members deepen and scale their social impact by offering tailored stipends (up to three years, if needed, for full-time dedication), customized engagement opportunities, increased visibility, and access to a global community of peers. Ashoka is looking for individuals who champion "new patterns of social good" and contribute to an "Everyone a Changemaker" world.

The selection process is anchored by five key criteria:

  • New Idea: A truly novel solution or approach to a social problem that can change the pattern in a field.
  • Creativity: Evidenced by both visionary goal-setting and innovative problem-solving.
  • Entrepreneurial Quality: Total absorption and relentless determination to implement the new idea and make it a new societal norm.
  • Social Impact of the Idea: The potential for significant, systemic change at a national or even global level, not just local service.
  • Ethical Fiber: Unquestionable trustworthiness and integrity, crucial for influencing societal change.
