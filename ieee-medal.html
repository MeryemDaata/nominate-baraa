IEEE Medal of Honor

About the Award

Established in 1917 to recognize key advances in radio technology, the IEEE Medal of Honor is now the Institute’s highest accolade and one of the most esteemed distinctions in science and technology worldwide.

The Prize

Gold medal, bronze replica, certificate, and beginning 2025 a USD 2 million prize shared among recipients.

Scope

Awarded to an individual or team (up to 3) for exceptional contributions or extraordinary careers in technology, engineering, or science within IEEE’s fields of interest.

Basis for Judging

Significance of achievement, originality, impact on society, impact on the profession, publications & patents, and overall quality of the nomination.

Nomination Process

Forms due 15 June; endorsement letters due 1 July.
Extended 2025 deadline: 28 July.
