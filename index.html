☀️ Organizer's Statement

I started this project because I know what it feels like to be passionate about data but feel underprepared after completing formal programs and felt the same way even after a four-year degree. Discovering Data with Baraa gave me clarity, direction, and the confidence to believe that I could not only understand analytics, but succeed in it. This is more than just a nomination campaign; it's an acknowledgment of one of the few educators making high-quality learning accessible and life-changing — for free.

This campaign is our collective thank you. We’re learning, growing, and organizing — together.
